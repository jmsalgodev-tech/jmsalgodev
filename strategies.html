Golden Cross Strategy

Trend Following • Forex & Crypto

Win Rate: 68% Avg Return: 12% Timeframe: 4H

Strategy Overview

This strategy identifies bullish and bearish market conditions using moving average crossovers. When the 50-period moving average crosses above the 200-period moving average, it generates a buy signal. Conversely, when the 50-period MA crosses below the 200-period MA, it generates a sell signal.

Key Features

  • Automated signal generation
  • Built-in risk management
  • Multi-timeframe analysis
  • Real-time alerts

⚠️ Risk Factors

  • Lagging indicator - may miss early signals
  • Whipsaws in sideways markets
  • Requires trend confirmation

RSI Divergence Strategy

Mean Reversion • Forex & Stocks

Win Rate: 72% Avg Return: 8% Timeframe: 1H

Strategy Overview

This strategy identifies potential reversal points by detecting divergences between price action and RSI momentum. When price makes higher highs but RSI makes lower highs (bearish divergence), it signals a potential reversal. The opposite is true for bullish divergence.

Key Features

  • Early reversal detection
  • Momentum analysis
  • Custom RSI parameters
  • Divergence confirmation

⚠️ Risk Factors

  • False divergence signals
  • Requires strong trend for accuracy
  • May generate late signals

Grid Trading Strategy

Range Trading • Crypto & Forex

Win Rate: 65% Avg Return: 10% Timeframe: 4H

Strategy Overview

This strategy places buy and sell orders at predetermined price levels (grid) to profit from price oscillations within a range. When price moves up, it sells at higher grid levels. When price moves down, it buys at lower grid levels.

Key Features

  • Automated grid placement
  • Range-bound market optimization
  • Risk distribution
  • Continuous operation

⚠️ Risk Factors

  • Trending market losses
  • Capital requirements
  • Grid level optimization

Breakout Strategy

Momentum • Forex & Stocks

Win Rate: 62% Avg Return: 18% Timeframe: 1H

Strategy Overview

This strategy identifies and trades breakouts from key support and resistance levels. It waits for price to break above resistance or below support with increased volume, then enters in the direction of the breakout.

Key Features

  • Key level identification
  • Volume confirmation
  • False breakout filtering
  • Momentum analysis

⚠️ Risk Factors

  • False breakout signals
  • Whipsaw losses
  • Late entry risks
